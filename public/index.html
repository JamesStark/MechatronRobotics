About Us

Who we are

Mechatron RoboticsTM is an educational division of Neurapses Technologies Pvt. Ltd. and has been providing STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering & Mathematics) based Courses especially ROBOTICS to school & college Students in India since 2013. We have trained more than 10,000 students across India though various trainings and workshops. Our training programs are designed to gain practical knowledge required as per industry standards which helps the students in establishing a suitable career right from the start.


Our Parent company Neurapses Technologies Pvt. Ltd. is the technology backbone of Mechatron RoboticsTM and holds expertise in the domain of Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning (ML) for businesses across the globe. We are among the very few to work in this future technology. Some of our business solutions have been Information extraction solutions, Prediction solutions & Big Data Analytics.
