 1. Workshop Overview :
The workshop would cover the basics of Robotics, Mechanics and Electronics and give an overview of Robotics Concepts to students with Hands on training. At the end of the workshop a small competition will be organised among the participated students.
      2. Course Overview:
         Day 1 & Day 2
  • Hands on training to build circuits
  • Bread-boarding techniques
  • Circuit making
  • Use of Multimeter
  • Use of Transistors, ICs etc
  • Build Interesting Electronic Projects
  • Build 2 wheel drive Robot
  • Make Remote and its connections

      Day 3 & Day 4
  • Introduction to Arduino
  • Learn Arduino Programming
  • Interfacing of Sensors
  • Make Arduino Based Projects
  • Make Arduino based Robot
  • Robotics Competition on the Circuits & Robots










3. Eligible Age Group: 7 Yrs. to 22 Yrs.
